Introduction

     The E-Waste Calculator Tool is a great way to see how much E-Waste you will contain. All you have to do is input how many electronics you have below and the E-Waste Calculator Tool calculates the total E-Waste you will have from all of your electronics. The tool helps put into into perspective just how much of a change you can make by identifying how much electronic mass you actually have.
